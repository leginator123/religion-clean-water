The Global Water Crisis

Water wasting has become a big issue and also with the recent spike of population, it was said to be hard to satisfy the needs of clean water for everyone... Until CWFE was founded.

Why This Matters

Everybody deserves to live and flourish and children shouldn't be thinking about providing water to the house, they should be thinking about their education and having a good childhood. Me having a childhood without the need of hunting for water and some people not having that luxury doesn't sit right with me.

Who is Affected?

High density and low resource / hot countries face the most issues. Also countries with diseases going around. e.g. India, Nigeria, Sudan to name a few.
