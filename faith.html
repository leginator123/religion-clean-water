Faith in Action

Living out Catholic Social Teachings

Catholic Social Teachings

We are called to support every life just because some people don't have any affect on our lives doesn't mean we shouldn't care for them. We are called to work together to help the poor.

Key Principles We Follow

Protect life and dignity of humans

We do this by giving water and help people survive which makes families happy and flourish.

Help the poor and vunerable

We find poor communities that need urgent help where they don't have any option because maybe their environment is really bad and there are lots of diseases and we give them a choice to pick living.

Rights and Responsibilities

Every innocent human has the right to live.

Solidarity

We're connecting communities that have water and don't have water so the people that we helped also help the people that need help by maybe sharing their water.

This isn't just charity, it's justice.

We are just in giving water to only the people in desperate need even if it means taking water from regions with lots of water. If we don't do it no one will...
